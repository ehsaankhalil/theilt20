Introduction

The International League T20 (ILT20) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league contested by teams from various countries around the world. It is one of the premier cricket tournaments globally, known for its high-octane matches and star-studded lineups.

History

The ILT20 was established in 2023 with the vision of promoting cricket on an international scale and providing a platform for players from different nations to showcase their talent. Over the years, it has gained immense popularity among cricket enthusiasts worldwide.
